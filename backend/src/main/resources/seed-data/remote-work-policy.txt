Remote Work Policy (Sample Internal Document)

1. Eligibility
All full-time employees may request remote work. Approval is at the discretion of the department head and must align with role requirements.

2. Core Hours
Remote staff are expected to be available during core hours 10:00–15:00 in their local timezone for meetings and collaboration.

3. Equipment
The company will provide a laptop and monitor. A one-time stipend is available for home office setup upon approval.

4. Security
All work must be done on company-issued devices or approved secure environments. Use of VPN is mandatory when accessing internal systems.

5. Data Privacy
We collect only the data necessary for payroll, benefits, and compliance. No internal documents are shared with third parties without legal review.

6. Review
This policy is reviewed annually.
